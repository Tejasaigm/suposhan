Samskruti Foundation-MIDHANI-Request to host, lead and conduct College Level-SUPOSHAN Program - Faculty Members-contribution to SUPOSHAN-Reg

SUPOSHAN, which stands for "Strategy for Undertaking Participatory Outreach by Students for Health and Nutrition," is dedicated to empowering students in various activities related to holistic food and nutrition. These activities enable students to cultivate a healthy and nutrition-conscious lifestyle that resonates with the well-being of their family members. Furthermore, students have the opportunity to enhance their competencies in social entrepreneurship, project management, change management, effective communication, and other vital skills. This holistic approach strengthens their portfolios and enriches their overall educational experience.
